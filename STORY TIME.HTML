Novels

DEF:

a fictitious prose narrative of book length, typically representing character and action with some degree of realism.

Horror

DEF:

Horror is a genre intended to make the audience feel fear, dread, disgust and unease. Unlike thrillers which focus on suspenseand mystery, horror aims to provoke visceral reactions by showing the disturbing and the macabre.

Poetry

DEF:

is simple or complex stanzaic verse-forms may be used, but there is usually a large cast, multiple voices, dialogue, narration, description, and action in a novelistic manner.

Drama

DEF:

The drama genre is strongly based in a character, or characters, that are in conflict at a crucial moment in their lives. Most dramas revolve around families and often have tragic or painful resolutions.

Fantasy

DEF:

A fantasy is something you imagine, which might involve dragons, unicorns, or an imaginary best friend. If you live in a fantasy world, you're not worrying much about reality-pleasant, maybe, but not very practical. Fantasy is dreams and imagination.

Romance

DEF:

A romance novel or romantic novel is a genre fiction novel that primary focuses on the relationship and romantic love between two people Romance novels encourage escapism from everyday life.

Adventure

DEF:

Adventure fiction is a type of fiction that usually presents danger, or gives the reader a sense of excitement. Some adventure fiction also satisfies the literary definition of romance fiction.

New adult

DEF:

New adult readers are typically aged 18-25 and adult fiction is aimed at anyone over the age of 18.It falls between the categories of young adult fiction.
